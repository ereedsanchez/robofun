Robofun Summer 2016

Gorillerrr: A game based on Frogger

 

 

 

 

Challenge:

Create and program a bluetooth controlled walking gorilla that tries to cross road without getting hit by robot vehicles.  This is a game inspired by the Video Game FROGGER.

 

 

 

Blueprints needed:

  • Buddy Bot
  • Gorilla Bot

Concepts that should be covered:

  • Piece ID
  • What is a robot?
  • What are the inputs and outputs?
  • What does a motor do?
  • Bluetooth - sending and receiving messages

Prior to starting:

  • Have EV3 bricks paired via bluetooth.  Label both EV3 bricks: Gorilla 1 / Controller 1, Gorilla 2 / Controller 2, etc..
  • Review bluetooth guide

 

How the game Works

Students will controll Gorilla robot with a bluetooth controller, and try to cross the “road”.

2-3 other buddy bots will move back and forth. The Buddy Bots will have a sensor that will make them reverse direction when they hit a wall.  

If Gorilla gets across without getting hit he wins.  If he gets hit by Buddy Bot he losses.

Sensors and Messages

A sensor is a device that detects, or senses, a signal. Most sensors are electrical or electronic, and are based on our 5 senses.  

What are our 5 senses?  

Based on our 5 Senses what kind of sensors do you think we can use?  

Touch(Button, Button on Brick/Bluetooth),

(Sight) Ultrasonic, Sound

These sensors, when they sense something (touch, sound, see, etc), they send a message to the computer - or the Brain.  The Computer then tells the robot to do something.  

 

Messages can be sent in many ways.  

How do you send messages? (Notes, Talking, Email)

 

Robots send electronic messages.  How do you think they send messages?  

Messages can be sent through a WIRE, or with the Gorilla we will be sending messages using BLUETOOTH. 

 

Does anyone know what bluetooth is, or where we use it?  

 

Bluetooth is a wireless way to send messages over the air.  It uses radio waves. These are the same waves that we use to send wifi for internet, or when we listen to the radio. Common uses of bluetooth are sending audio to headphones or speakers.  One great thing about bluetooth is does not use a lot of power, and we can control it, we decide what messages we are going to send.
